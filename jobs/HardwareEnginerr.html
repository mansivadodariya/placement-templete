Hardware Design Engineers:

Below we've compiled a list of the most important skills for a hardware design engineer. We ranked the top skills based on the percentage of hardware design engineer resumes they appeared on. For example, 7.1% of hardware design engineer resumes contained rf as a skill. Let's find out what skills a hardware design engineer actually needs in order to be successful in the workplace.

  • The most common hard skill for a hardware design engineer is rf. 7.1% hardware design engineers have this skill on their resume.

  • The second most common hard skill for a hardware design engineer is pcb appearing on 6.2% of resumes. The third most common is analog on 6.1% of resumes.

  • Three common soft skills for a hardware design engineer are analytical skills, problem-solving skills and communication skills.
