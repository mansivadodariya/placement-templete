Free Job Posting Sites:

Rank Job Board Description
1. Indeed The highest-trafficked job board in the U.S. Allows totally free job postings, with the option to boost visibility with pay-per-click ads. It is often the first place businesses go to post a job ad for free.
2. Google for Jobs Jobs posted online can be included in Google search results if they are formatted correctly or posted to job boards that are indexed by Google.
3. Jora Jora is a general job posting site that is similar to Indeed. The global job board offers employers up to 10 free job posts per month.
4. Handshake This career network site helps employers connect with students and young alumni from over 1,400 educational institutions.
5. Wellfound Wellfound, formerly AngelList Talent, offers free job postings that reach people in the tech and startup world. The site provides access to an audience of more than 8 million candidates and features advanced search filters, candidate messaging tools, and an applicant tracking system.
6. LinkedIn LinkedIn has free and paid posting options. Employers can also conduct a manual search for potential candidates and get in touch with them for free if their network is big enough.
7. Ladders Another great place to list jobs online for free. The site focuses on jobs that pay over $100,000 and offers one free post per month. Users must purchase promoted job ads to list additional jobs.
8. Chegg Internships A U.S.-based, free job posting site with a focus on paid internship opportunities and entry-level jobs for students and graduates across the U.S.
9. Learn4Good This free job posting site lets employers post up to 20 jobs for free over six months. Employers can post all 20 at once.
10. PostJobFree As implied by the name, PostJobFree allows employers to advertise their vacancies without charge. As one of the first free job posting sites, it ranks well on Google for many job searches.
11. Hubstaff Talent Hubstaff Talent is a remote job board that specializes in connecting employers with freelancers across the globe. The platform is completely free and distributes job posts to other sites for maximum reach.
12. Jobvertise The site caters primarily to the U.S. and Canada but also features job listings for vacancies in Europe, Asia, and beyond. Employers have access to a free standard plan that includes basic job postings and resume searches.
13. startupers startupers is a Craigslist-style job posting site for tech companies. Posting is free, and every listing is reviewed by a startupers employee to prevent spam.
14. JobSpider JobSpider is a job board similar to Craigslist. The platform is easy to use and offers a free job posting service as well as free resume database access.
15. Jobomas Jobomas is a global job portal that was founded in Mexico. It has a dedicated U.S. website and offers 30-day job postings, three resume downloads, and up to 10 contacts via WhatsApp in its free plan.
16. Jobisite A general job site with free postings, but less traffic than Jora and Indeed. The posting process is simple and users have free access to a resume database.
17. JobInventory JobInventory.com is a completely free general job search site. Posts don't expire and can be removed once the position is filled.
18. ReliefWeb ReliefWeb, a website maintained by the United Nations, offers free humanitarian job postings.
