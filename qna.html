  • What Is a Work Placement?

    A work placement for students refers to a monitored or supervised work schedule in an organisation. It allows students to experience the real-time “job” in a company.
    Almost always, an employer seeking to fill a vacant post for a particular job, asks for “experience,” apart from the required qualifications.
    Finding that perfect job just after graduation can be difficult for students without work experience. That is why we stress the importance of work placements for students.
